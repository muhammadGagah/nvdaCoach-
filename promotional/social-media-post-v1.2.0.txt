--- LONG FORM (LinkedIn / Facebook) ---

Learning a screen reader is hard. Not because the commands are complicated —
but because there's always a gap between reading about a command and actually
using one.

NVDA Coach closes that gap.

It's a free add-on for the NVDA screen reader that teaches commands through
short, guided practice sessions — from inside NVDA itself. No PDFs. No videos.
No switching windows to follow along. Press NVDA+Shift+C and the Coach starts
walking you through what to do and why, one step at a time.

Version 1.2.0 is out now — 35 lessons across 5 chapters, including a brand-new
opening lesson that answers the question every new screen reader user has but
rarely gets a straight answer to:

"Who's actually in charge of this key — Windows, the app, or the screen reader?"

That one concept changes how everything else clicks into place.

Completely free. No account. No internet required after install.

Download it here:
https://github.com/tonygeb23/nvdaCoach-/releases/download/v1.2.0/nvdaCoach-1.2.0.nvda-addon

If you work with someone who's learning NVDA — a student, a client, a family
member — send them this link. It's the structured starting point I wish existed
when I started teaching.
